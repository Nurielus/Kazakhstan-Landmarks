Medeu is a world-famous high-altitude ice skating rink located near Almaty, Kazakhstan. Built in 1972 at an elevation of 1,691 meters, it has hosted numerous international competitions and is known for its high-quality ice, thanks to pure mountain water. Medeu is a favorite spot for both professional athletes and visitors who enjoy skating in a breathtaking alpine setting. In winter, it becomes a hub for winter sports, while in summer, it serves as a starting point for hikes to Shymbulak and the surrounding mountains. Its combination of sports history and natural beauty makes it a must-visit destination.
